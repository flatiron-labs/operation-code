About the Scholarships

Call us crazy, but we think education should be 100% focused on outcomes. The Learn-Verified Web Developer program is not designed to be easy. It's not for dipping your toes in the water, and it's not for dabbling in new skills. There are plenty of resources online to help you do that. Learn is designed to help you change your life.

With Learn you will build real software using the same tools and workflows professional software developers use every day. Our market-aligned curriculum, provided by NYC’s Flatiron School, has already launched hundreds of careers. The material on Learn requires a tremendous amount of focus and dedication.

Rather than make it easy, we connect you with a community of Learners with whom you will struggle, learn and succeed.
