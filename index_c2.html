About

The Program

We care about giving veterans the opportunity to change their lives with technology. That’s why Flatiron School and Operation Code have partnered to offer $25,000 in scholarships for veterans to learn professional skills on Learn.

The first 50 accepted Operation Code applicants will receive $500 off Learn's monthly tuition. Participants will experience an immersive, online platform with real-time help from Learn Experts. The Full Stack Web Development program comes with a job offer guarantee.

Eligibility

  • Veterans
  • Residing in the United States
  • Access to a Mac or PC
  • Interested in web development
  • No prior experience required

    • Background

    Flatiron School: Flatiron School trains passionate, creative people in web and mobile development. We bring together people with diverse perspectives and backgrounds who want to change the world with code.

    Operation Code: At Operation Code, we believe every Veteran and their families deserve access to 21st century software development education and mentorship. We believe Veterans equipped with programming knowledge will create web products and services that improve and move the United States forward and make a better world.

    How It Works

    1. Fill in the information above. You will be guided through the steps to set up your computer for Learn.

    2. Get to work! The 30-hour qualifying track is entirely free—it's a great way to figure out if programming is something you'll enjoy. It's not easy, but Learn Experts will be there to help!

    3. Apply for the program. Once you've finished the qualifying track, you'll fill out a short application to tell us about yourself. The first 50 Operation Code applicants accepted will receive a scholarship for Learn's Full Stack Web Development program.

    4. Learn. Check out the Full Stack Web Development syllabus. You'll be learning online as part of a community, working on real projects, and interacting with people in real time. We estimate that the curriculum represents 600-800 hours of work.

    5. Get a job as a web developer. Flatiron School recognizes your ability to succeed. Scholarship recipients who complete the program will receive a job offer within six months — this is guaranteed, or we'll give a full tuition refund.
